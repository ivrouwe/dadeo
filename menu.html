Menu

Try our unique Cajun-style cuisine.

DaDeO offers an extensive menu to satisfy meat-lovers, vegetarians, and sweet tooths alike. Enjoy our signature biscuits with jalapeno jelly. They're a great way to whet your appetite for some southern-style cooking.

Our menu items are available for takeout so you can enjoy your meal in our retro diner or order it to go. To place an order or to make a reservation, please contact us.
